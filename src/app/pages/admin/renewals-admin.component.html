Prompt Tips

  • • Use ## Section Headers to define the output structure — the AI will follow them
  • • Be specific about what product recommendations you want (e.g., "recommend Cisco Catalyst 9000 series for ISR replacements")
  • • Include instructions for timeline prioritization to help sales teams act on the most urgent items first
  • • The AI receives the customer's full hardware/software inventory, architecture breakdown, and timeline data automatically
  • • Mention specific Cisco programs or promotions you want the AI to reference
  • • Ask for ROI justification or business case language if needed for customer presentations
